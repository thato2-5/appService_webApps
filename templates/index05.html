Beware Latest Telegram Scam

The Financial Sector Conduct Authority FSCA has warned the public to be vigilant of a scam being run on Telegram, in which individuals are impersonating Freedom Capital Investment Managers and Victoria Leigh Cook.
According to the information received by the FSCA, these individuals claim to offer investment opportunities in cryptocurrency trading, forex trading and gold mining, promising substantial profits. There are three supported trading plans:

  1. Beginners Plan
  2. Premium Plan
  3. VIP plan

Investors can earn eight times their initial capital within six days.

The FSCA has learnt that the scam operates by requiring clients to select a trading plan, purchase bitcoins to fund a trading account and deposit funds using a provided address.
The scammers then trade on behalf of the clients for a 10% commission on the total profit.

Petrol Prices Surge

July 14, 2024 by Economics Club

The price of petrol in South Africa has seen a significant increase, impacting consumers and businesses alike. Experts attribute the surge to a combination of factors, including global oil prices and local economic conditions.

With the cost of living already high, the rising fuel prices are expected to place additional financial strain on households and small businesses. The government has announced measures to alleviate some of the burden, but the long-term outlook remains uncertain.
